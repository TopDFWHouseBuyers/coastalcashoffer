Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you close on my Southern California home? +
We can close in as few as 7 days. If you need more time, we work around your schedule — even if that means 60 or 90 days. California escrow typically takes longer but we move as fast as you need.
Do I need to make repairs before selling? +
Never. We buy houses in any condition — foundation issues, fire damage, mold, outdated systems, code violations. You don't spend a single dollar on repairs or cleaning in California's expensive contractor market.
Is your cash offer really no obligation? +
100%. You can request an offer, hear our number, and walk away with no pressure and no cost. California sellers deserve options — we'd rather you make the right decision than feel pushed.
How do you calculate your offer on a California home? +
We analyze the property's condition, location, and recent comparable sales in your area. We factor in repair costs we'll handle after purchase. We're fully transparent — we show you exactly how we arrived at the number.
Do you buy homes with tenants in California? +
Yes. We buy California properties with tenants in place all the time. You don't have to navigate California's complex tenant protection laws or wait for leases to expire. We handle that after closing.
Will I really pay zero fees and commissions? +
Yes. No agent commission (typically 5–6%), no closing costs, no inspection fees. In a California transaction that can save you $50,000+ on a $1M home. What we offer is exactly what you walk away with.
